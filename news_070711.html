    WindStream Technologies, Inc was honored to include its TurboMill™ micro-wind turbine as one of two featured products at U.S. Ambassador Charles H. Rivkin's residence in Paris, France during Independence Day celebrations.

    Ambassador Charles H. Rivkin and his wife Susan Tolson welcomed 3000 guests to a reception to celebrate the 235th anniversary of American Independence. The Chevy Volt and WindStream Technologies' TurboMill™ were the two featured products showcased at the celebration.

    The theme for the celebration was 'California Caché'. Guests were treated to perfomances by American soprano Renée Fleming and stars from the hit American TV show 'Glee'.
