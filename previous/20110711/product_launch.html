  • WindStream to Release New Product Soon

    The company plans to introduce its TurboMill™ device for sale in Q1 2011.

    NEW ALBANY, IN. - WindStream Technologies Inc., maker of highly efficient and low-cost micro-wind turbines for urban and off-grid environments, has announced that the company plans to deliver its first deployment of TurboMills. The initial deliveries and installations will be made to the company's international pre-order clients, as part of a larger waiting list to customers.

    The company has embarked on more than three years of engineering, testing, and optimization of its wind energy device, working with a global network of innovation and supply partners. The Indiana-based company plans to assemble the devices in the USA, as well as offer the devices for foreign export.

    "We're very excited that after months of dilligent R&D we're ready to show the world what our TurboMills can do," said Dan Bates, WindStream Technologies CEO. "We're confident it will satisfy our customers' needs and impress the industry as we set a new standard for micro wind turbines going forward."

    After orders from the company's waiting list are delivered, devices will be made avaliable to domestic and international customers. To inquire about the TurboMill device, please contact the company (news@windstream-inc.com).

    About Windstream Technologies, Inc.

    WindStream Technologies Inc., headquartered in New Albany, Indiana., designs, manufactures and markets low-cost, highly efficient micro-wind turbines for urban, suburban and off-grid environments. Founded in July 2008, WindStream Technologies has been perfecting and testing, and now manufacturing its patented TurboMill micro-wind energy generation device. The company aims to become the leading distributed energy generation solution for municipalities, light industry, residential/consumer, and developing nation applications. The company is currently deploying Phase I pilot installations with select partners across the United States. For more information about WindStream Technologies, Inc., please visit www.windstream-inc.com
