    TurboMill™ is a highly efficient, low-cost micro-wind turbine for urban and off-grid environments. Each unit is no larger than a standard satellite TV dish and easy to install.

    Affordable

    Our solutions offer the lowest up front and operating costs, leading to fastest time to payback.

    • Lowest cost per watt
    • Lower purchase price
    • Minimal operation costs

    Efficient

    Our turbines are designed to generate energy in low wind conditions, a gentle breeze can produce power.

    • Operates at low speed wind
    • Wide operating wind speed
    • Low drag vertical-axis blade design

    Simple

    Plug and play design makes for a simple installation.

    • Easy to install
    • Small size and minimal footprint
    • Scalable

    Safe

    Environmentally friendly device, poses no danger to people or wildlife...

    • Quiet operation
    • Zero emissions for energy generation
    • Safe for wildlife
